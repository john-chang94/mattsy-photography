About Me


Growing up in Los Angeles as an immigrant, I was fortunate enough to experience the traditions and customs of my Asian heritage as well as American culture. I discovered how photography allowed me to capture treasured moments and express how I see the world around me. Working on weddings, I love the story of how people from different backgrounds and ethnicities come together as one. For me, it's about connecting with people and telling their story by capturing the beauty and emotion of the day through photography.
